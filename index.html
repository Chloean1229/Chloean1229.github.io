From 2020 to Now

Now I am in my first semester of the Strategic Product Design. Before college I was full of desire to create beautiful things, making models and painting was a process of creating beauty. This is what design brings to me initially. Then I majored in product design in my undergraduate studies. After doing some design projects, I realized that design is not just about looking good, it aims to solve practical problems. I also found that in many cases a perfect product alone cannot solve complex issues as design is connected to so many disciplines. It is necessary to stand on a more systematic and long-term perspective, such as strategic design or service design, to come up with design solutions and seek collaboration with other stakeholders.

Last year, I came to know the concept of T-type Talent when I listened to a lecture. If the learning of the undergraduate phase equipped me with “|” (i.e. the research of Product Design itself and its involving fields), then I hope to also have “-”. In this way, I will be able to gain access to more broad knowledge about service design, sociology and commercial strategy, so as to design works with an eye to the interests of the whole.
